About Me

Hey there, I am Stephan, I am from Switzerland and I currently work as a Game Developer in Hamburg, Germany. A job I very much like so far.

A couple of years ago everything looked so much different then now. I just started my studies in History and was trying to find my one true passion. Well to be honest I got lost, probably somewhere between Caesar and Churchill, and my priorities shifted from learning about the past to creating awesome stuff and building the future. Though, apart from the fact that I have to answer the same question over and over again - maybe I will find the time to answer it here once and for all, I never regretted my choices and still cary my BA of Arts with honor.

I always liked games - like a lot - because games are fun and they teach you a lot. And now I build them. Meaning: I don’t do graphics or game design or make things move around on your screen. It is just that part of games which is not visible and really hard to describe, like building an AI. Oh man, AI’s are really tricky. Apart from thinking about how to make AI’s event better I am crazy about Clean Code and love to try out all the cool new stuff especially things related to nodejs and databases. Unfortunately my days are usually way to short to keep up with all the things I would love to but I am probably not they only one with such a problem.

So yes, that is me.
